About DOJO

Where Strength Meets Discipline

Founded in 2010, DOJO has been the premier destination for serious strength athletes and fitness enthusiasts alike. Our facility is designed to help you push your limits in a supportive, no-nonsense environment.

We believe in the fundamentals: squats, deadlifts, bench presses, and overhead presses. But we also incorporate innovative techniques to help you break through plateaus and achieve new personal records.

Our coaches are all competitive lifters with years of experience helping athletes of all levels reach their goals.
